An inventory list provides a clear and organized account of everything of value. All items listed here are from 33 Spring Rd and 12785 Mariner Ct. Wolfeboro items are not included. The goal is to be as thorough as possible, as even seemingly inconsequential items can add up.

Determine using Replacement Cost vs. Actual Cash Value

Replacement cost is the amount it would take to replace or repair an item with a like kind and quality, without deducting for depreciation. Actual cash value (ACV) is the replacement cost with depreciation taken into account.
